Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Our Privacy Philosophy

Privacy by Design

momento is built on the principle of intentional simplicity, and this extends to your privacy. We believe your meditation practice is deeply personal, and we've designed our app to respect that completely.

This Privacy Policy explains how momento ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our meditation app. Our commitment is simple: your privacy is not just protected—it's prioritized.

Information We Don't Collect

momento is designed to be completely private and offline. Here's what we don't collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Meditation session data or usage patterns
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Social media connections
  • Audio recordings of your sessions
  • Health or wellness data

How momento Works Offline

Fully Offline Experience

momento operates entirely offline on your device. All meditation content, including binaural beats and session timers, are stored locally on your device. No internet connection is required for any functionality.

Local Data Storage

Any preferences you set (such as session duration or meditation mode preferences) are stored only on your device using local storage mechanisms provided by your operating system. This data never leaves your device.

No Account Required

momento doesn't require you to create an account, sign up, or provide any personal information. Simply download and start your practice immediately.

App Store Information

When you download momento from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, those platforms may collect information about your download and usage according to their own privacy policies. We do not have access to this information.

App store reviews and ratings are managed by Apple and Google respectively. We may read public reviews to improve our app, but we cannot identify individual users from these reviews.

Technical Information

Crash Reports

If momento crashes, your device's operating system may generate crash reports. These reports contain technical information about the crash but no personal data. We may receive anonymized crash reports through app store developer tools to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

App Updates

App updates are delivered through the App Store or Google Play. We don't track who downloads updates or when they're installed.

No Third-Party Services

momento does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. There are no embedded tracking pixels, cookies, or external connections of any kind.

Data Security

Since momento doesn't collect or transmit any personal data, there's no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. Your meditation practice remains completely private to you.

Any local preferences stored on your device are protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and access controls.

Children's Privacy

momento is suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for users under 13 years of age. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is fully protected when using momento.

Your Rights and Control

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights (like access, deletion, or portability) don't apply in the usual sense. However, you have complete control over momento:

  • Local Data: You can clear all local app data through your device settings
  • App Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device
  • Complete Privacy: Your meditation practice remains entirely private to you

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (which would only happen if we add new features), we'll update this policy and the version number. Since momento doesn't collect contact information, we can't notify users directly of changes. We recommend checking this page periodically if you have concerns about privacy practices.

Any changes will maintain our commitment to user privacy and will never introduce tracking or data collection without a compelling user benefit and clear disclosure.

International Users

momento's privacy-first design means it complies with privacy regulations worldwide, including GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection laws. Since we don't collect personal data, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.
